How to use

An Application to increasing your knowledge about Insect World
Click on start application to start the application. There is three option against
a picture of Insect you have to choose the correct option among the option given.
If your answer is correct, there is a green right symbol against your answer.
If the answer you given is wrong, displayable symbol is a red cross and the right answer.
At the bottom of the application there is a next button to go to the next question of the application.
